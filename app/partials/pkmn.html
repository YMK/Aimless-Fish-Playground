Welcome to the DraftLocke generator. This was inspired by a discussion on Reddit about a new form of NuzLocke. The idea being that you would use a pack of TCG cards to define what pokemon you used in the locke. As this would get costly if you ended up doing this several times, it was brought up that there could be an online generator for that. So, I decided to make one.

Currently, the generator will give you 6 pokemon from whatever generations you choose
